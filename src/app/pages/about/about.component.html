Hi, I'm Michelle Baamonde.
(think "bah-mon-day"!)

Equipped with a communications degree from NYU, I joined IPSY’s customer care team in the SF Bay Area, focusing on social media support. A few years later, I returned to NYC and joined the brand partnerships team, where I managed communications for 10-12 brand accounts per month.

Coming from a background where I prioritized the needs of the customers, clients, and business, I focus on the user at every stage of my design process. I am intrigued by how research uncovers how people think and drives design decisions that bring ideas to life. My goal is to work with a team where I can use my communication and design skills to build products and meaningful experiences for users.

Outside of design, I love to run in Central Park with my rescue dog Milo (goal is to run a sub-2 hour marathon) and play video games. You can sometimes catch me live streaming on Twitch!
